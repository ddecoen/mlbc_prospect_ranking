Nutrition tips

Protein first at every meal
Chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, legumes. Protein keeps you full and protects the muscle you're building.
Don't slash calories too hard
Going below 1,600–1,700 cal backfires — you lose muscle, feel terrible, and stall. Moderate deficit wins long-term.
Watch liquid calories
Lattes, juice, and dressings are the biggest hidden sources. Swapping these alone can create a meaningful deficit.
Track for 2–3 weeks only
Just long enough to calibrate your sense of portions — then you'll know intuitively what a reasonable day looks like.
